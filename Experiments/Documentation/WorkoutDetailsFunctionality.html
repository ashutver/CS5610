Workout Details Functionality

Workout Details page shows details about a workout. Workout will be contain all your exercises that user intends to perform during a week. Days will be listed and all exercises will be listed for a particular day.


Snapshot

Workout Details Services

Workout Details services are designed using Node.js and Express Module. MongoDB is used for storing all the user data. Following services are are playoing role on login page:

  1. Fetch workout details.
  2. Add/Delete a workout day.
  3. Add/Delete a exercise.
