Muscle Functionality

Muscle page displays graphically all major body muscles. All muscles are listed and as user hovers mouse over any muscle then that muscle is shown in body along with exercises to target that muscle. Things that are shown are:

  • Muscle name
  • Graphical representation of body muscle
  • Related Exercies
